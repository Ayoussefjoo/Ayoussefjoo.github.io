Relief and Human Rights

Vision

Refugee affairs among members of Arab communities

Investing in generations with intellectual, cultural and civilizational rehabilitation for the children of Arab communities in Western societies, through the use of the latest technological, educational and educational systems, and not only providing health and food assistance to them . Coexistence, mutual respect and upholding the values of humanity are the desired goals; Civilizations converge and do not collide.

About Us

The Arab Organization for Relief and Human Rights is one of the non-governmental civil society organizations working in Greece and the European Union, and it specializes in refugee affairs among members of Arab communities . It is based on a working group composed of a different group of people that specializes in providing distinctive, meaningful content and a different vision.

Services

Family reunion

Family reunion from middle eastern countries to Greece.

Asylum documents

Transactions and Asylum document extraction through media and news coverage via the Channel of the RMC Refugee Media Center

Psychological rehabilitation

Psychological and educational rehabilitation affected by war

Educational services

Providing educational services and enrolling students in Greek schools.

Merger projects

Cooperation with the Ministry of Immigration and Asylum in the implementation of integration projects for refugees and the definition of refugees of nature and customs of Greek society

Translation services

Providing translation services to refugees in hospitals and government agencies
