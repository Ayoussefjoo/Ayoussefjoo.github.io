Vigil in front of the EUROPEAN Union headquarters in Athens

The brothers and sisters of the refugees in Greece inform you that, God willing, a vigil will be held in front of the European Union headquarters on Wednesday,May 27, 2012, at Syndingma Square at 12:00 p.m. to demand that the refugees be released from the payroll system, financial assistance and housing, and are scheduled to leave on 31 May 2020 and the second section scheduled to leave on 31 July 2020.

3- To remove the refugees affected by the wars in the Greek islands and reception centers as soon as possible and to stop the exit of other nationalities that are not affected by the wars until these centers are free of war.
4- Stop all financial assistance and housing for refugees who are not affected by wars except in special cases where this aid is tampered with, resulting in it not reaching its beneficiaries.
5- Work to find alternative solutions to receive refugees who are in Greece in the European Union countries, whether it is a serious resettlement program or breaking dublin's stamp on Greek refugees.
6- Call for a meeting of the parties concerned with the refugee crisis in Greece: representatives of the Greek Ministry of Immigration, representatives of the European High Commissioner for Refugees, representatives of the High Commissioner for Refugees.
